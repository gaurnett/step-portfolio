About Me

Reggae has coursed through my veins from birth as my heart learned to beat to the sounds of Bob Marley and Chronixx. Being a musician has not only connected me to my history, but has showed me how to be expressive, allowing me to speak my own language through the strum of a string or the beat of drum. I was never a stranger to adventure, traveling to the beautiful Blue Mountains or swimming in the clear waters of the Caribbean Sea was something I always enjoyed. My socio-economic background has allowed me to have unique opinions on the matters that I may face. My opinions may be similar or completely unorthodox, but this only makes my relationship with my peers more exciting. Interested in finding out more? Reach out, I love lending a hand.
