I really started photography when I got my first camera from my dad. He's a professional photographer in Jamaica and he always had camera lying around. There were other times where I would get the old cameras whenever he bought new ones. My main subjects were bees, flowers and views of amazing sites across my country. When I moved to the states for school my passion continued. I'd start to take photos of people and continue to snap photos of other views and animals I'd come across. I was always that annoying friend who would ask to stop the car on roadtrips just to take a picture. I'm not the best but I still try to improve on every shot I take.
