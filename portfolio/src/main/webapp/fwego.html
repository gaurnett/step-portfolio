Personal Project: Fwego

Easily discover popular local and international events right in the palm of your hand. With Fwego, there’s always something new to do - whether it’s a stage show, party or a concert by your favourite artiste. Our interactive map allows you to visualize all the events that are going on around you and give you details about each one. All this allows you to never have to worry about where an event is being kept or how to get there.
